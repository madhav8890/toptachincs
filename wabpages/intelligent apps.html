Everyone is expecting decent opportunities in the form of experience as-a-service, with the evolving incorporation of technologies in the world of mobile. They are raising questions on the abilities of typical mobile apps while imploring a clear demand for a personalized experience.

Dispersion of this can be that mobile apps have begun embracing the capacities of AI to eventually emerge as Intelligent apps. Now you might be curious to know what are intelligent apps, so let’s see below.

What are Intelligent Apps?

Intelligent mobile apps are essentially AI-enabled applications that provide a rich, adaptive, and personalized user experience. These apps incorporate the power of predictive and prescriptive analytics, consumer data, cutting-edge technologies, and operational data with application development tools and the latest user-centric design to make a high-end user experience.

As we have covered what are intelligent apps, let’s have a glimpse on the intelligent apps examples to understand them better.

Is I-Apps’ future-ready?

Let’s look at intelligent app examples and the benefits of intelligent apps. So let’s get started.

Intelligent App examples

Elsa Speak

ELSA Speak is one of the popular Artificial Intelligence apps that assist users to learn English quickly and effectively. The application has a documented down test designed by the professionals which facilitate users to learn and speak English fluently in a smaller period. Furthermore, this Android and iOS app enable them to proceed further by keeping a track of their progress.

Cortana

Microsoft’s Cortana is one of the best Intelligent apps that is altering our lives. The personal assistant app proposes different features and functionalities to users, such as setting reminders, keeping track of important files and documents, getting insight into weather forecasts, etc

SwiftKey Keyboard

An AI-powered keyboard, SwiftKey Keyboard which auto-corrects your wrong text. Moreover, the app also proposes features like design, changing font colour, style, theme, etc, and enhancing your conversation by suggesting emojis.

Socratic

Socratic is an AI-based intelligent application that simplifies the learning process as it is introduced by Google. The application authorized students to inquire or ask questions either by asking verbally or by taking a photo of questions. The app itself comes up with an optimal question and answer sections, including YouTube videos, and links of the blog, which makes the process of studying quicker and simpler.

Hound

Hound is also another perfect example of how Artificial Intelligence in mobile apps can simplify your life. The app seizes voice commands to execute different actions such as getting familiar with the best hotel, restaurant, or doctors.

Another fascinating feature of this i-app is that it translates speech to text and vice-versa.

IRIS

IRIS, an example of the execution of artificial intelligence in mobile apps for adequate services. It is developed by Paint Technology Solutions. An intelligent reporting Android and iOS app for team leaders, managers, and co-workers of any specific project.

The application allows them to get access to the report of any team member. And the favourable part is that multiple users can access the same document at the same time and that too from distinct sync devices.

FaceApp

FaceApp is also another application that uses Neural and AI network technology to build freaky, hilarious, most realistic face transformations.

Youper

Youper is an easy computing Artificial intelligence app that caters to health and fitness issues and accessible on both Android and iOS. The application is established on cognitive behavioural therapy, motivates users to share their social phobia and get real-time stimulating advice. In this way, it enhances their social interactions and lifestyle effectively.

Flo

Flo is yet an i-app that is mainly used in the process of photo and video editing. The app appears with a built-in voice assistant that facilitates users to give a voice command regarding which footage to pick, and on what basis (based on date, location, or tags). And eventually, incorporate them optimally with high-quality music.

The application also assigns Machine learning algorithms that provide options like choosing the best moments, locations, detecting faces, etc.

Now, let’s look at the features that make these apps prominent.

Benefits of Intelligent Apps

Action-oriented

The major factor of i-apps is that these applications do not wait for users to make every move. Instead, they study user behaviour and provide personalized and actionable outcomes using the power of Predictive Analytics.

In this way, they cut down the conflict of users and urge them to seize desired actions.

The great example to understand this trait of Intelligent applications is Hound app. It indicates the best nearby restaurants, hotels, and more in real-time. This provides users with a push towards making a booking, which probably would not have been possible if they were asked to search manually.
